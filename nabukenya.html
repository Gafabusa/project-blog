Project Work

My Contributions

1

Project Ideation

I played a key role in the ideation phase of the Fall Armyworm Diagnosis project, contributing to the conceptualization and development of the application. By collaborating with my team members, I helped define the project scope, identify key features, and outline the user experience.

2

Field Data Collection

I was actively involved in the field data collection process, working closely with farmers, researchers, and agricultural experts to gather valuable insights on FAW infestations. This firsthand experience provided me with valuable data to inform the development of our application.

3

Community Section Development

One of my key contributions is the development of the Community Section in our application. This section will serve as a platform for farmers, researchers, and agricultural experts to connect, share knowledge, and collaborate on pest management strategies.

4

Image Upload and Scanning

I will work on the image upload and scanning feature that will allow users to upload images of their crops and scan them for signs of Fall Armyworm infestations using machine learning algorithms.

5

Map API Integration

I will integrate a Map API to show the distribution of Fall Armyworm infestations in Uganda. This feature will help farmers, researchers, and policymakers to identify high-risk areas and track the movement of pests across different regions of the country.

6

Data Design Documentation

I will work on the data design of the software design document, defining the data structures, storage mechanisms, and processing workflows to create a robust and scalable architecture for our application.
