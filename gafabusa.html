Project Work

My Contributions

1

Project Ideation

From the beginning, I actively encouraged my team to focus on a project related to agriculture, recognizing the critical role technology can play in improving farming practices. I strongly believed that leveraging software solutions for agriculture could have a meaningful impact on food security and farmers' livelihoods.

2

Field Data Collection

Beyond contributing to the project's idea formulation, I also actively participated in the data collection process. As a team, we gathered crucial data from NARO to ensure our solution was grounded in real agricultural challenges and expert insights.

3

Community Section Development

I am also going to work on the Community Section of our application, which will serve as an interactive space for farmers to share experiences, ask questions, and receive expert advice on FAW management.

4

FAW Distribution Map

As part of my contributions to this project, I will also be responsible for developing the FAW Distribution Map in our application. This feature will visually represent the spread of Fall Armyworm infestations across different districts in Uganda.

5

Model Training

In addition to my contributions to the Community Section and the FAW Distribution Map, I am also actively involved in training of the machine learning model for FAW diagnosis. My role will include preparing and cleaning the dataset, selecting suitable algorithms, and fine-tuning the model to ensure high accuracy in detecting FAW infestations.
