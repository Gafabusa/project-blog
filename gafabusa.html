About Me

I am Gafabusa Willy, a passionate advocate for sustainable agriculture and technology-driven solutions. I brought the idea of the Fall Armyworm Diagnosis project to focus on addressing the challenges faced by farmers in Uganda. My goal is to leverage technology to improve crop yields and ensure food security.

My Contributions

1

Project Ideation

I proposed the idea of focusing the project on agriculture, specifically addressing the Fall Armyworm (FAW) problem in Uganda. This idea was inspired by the need to help smallholder farmers combat FAW infestations effectively.

2

Field Data Collection

I actively participated in field data collection, visiting farms across Uganda to gather information on FAW prevalence, farmer practices, and the impact of FAW on maize crops.

3

Community Section Development

I am currently working on the community section of the app, which will allow farmers to share experiences, ask questions, and receive expert advice on FAW management.

4

FAW Distribution Map

I will also work on the map section of the app, which will visually display the distribution of FAW across Ugandan districts. This will help farmers and policymakers make informed decisions.
