Project Work

My Contributions

1

Project Ideation

I played a key role in the ideation phase of the Fall Armyworm Diagnosis project, contributing to the conceptualization and design of the application. Drawing on my experience in mobile development and machine learning, I collaborated with my team members to brainstorm innovative solutions for early pest detection and management.

2

Field Data Collection

I actively participated in the data collection process, which involved conducting research at NARO and visiting different farmers to gather firsthand insights on the impact of Fall Armyworm (FAW) infestations. During this process, I engaged with farmers to understand their challenges.

3

Community Section Development

I will also be actively involved in brainstorming and developing the community section of the mobile app, working closely with my fellow team members. Together, we'll explore ways to create a dynamic platform where farmers can connect, share experiences, and access valuable resources.

4

FAW Detection and Diagnosis

I will be responsible for developing the FAW detection and diagnosis module of the mobile application, leveraging my expertise in machine learning and image processing. By working on this component, I aim to create a robust and accurate system that can identify early signs of FAW infestations.

5

Training of the Model

I will also be involved in training the machine learning model that will power the Fall Armyworm Diagnosis application. Leveraging my expertise in data science and AI, I will work on developing algorithms that can accurately detect and classify FAW infestations based on image data. By training the model on a diverse dataset of crop images and pest samples, I aim to create a robust and reliable system.

6

System Architecture

I will be working on the system architecture section of the software design document. In this section, I will outline the overall structure and components of the system, detailing how the various parts of the software will interact with each other. I will describe the different layers of the application, including the frontend, backend, and data storage, as well as the flow of data between these components.
