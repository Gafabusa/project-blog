My Contributions

Project Ideation

I played a key role in the ideation phase of the Fall Armyworm Diagnosis project, contributing to the conceptualization and design of the application. Drawing on my experience in mobile development and machine learning, I collaborated with my team members to brainstorm innovative solutions for early pest detection and management. By combining our expertise in technology and agriculture, we developed a comprehensive plan to address the challenges faced by farmers in Uganda due to Fall Armyworm infestations. My contributions to the project's ideation process helped shape the vision and scope of our application, ensuring that it meets the needs of farmers and agricultural stakeholders.

Field Data Collection

I actively participated in the data collection process, which involved conducting research at NARO (National Agricultural Research Organization) and visiting different farmers to gather firsthand insights on the impact of Fall Armyworm (FAW) infestations. During this process, I engaged with farmers to understand their challenges, current pest management practices, and their awareness of technology-driven solutions. The field visits provided valuable real-world data that will be crucial in shaping the development of the mobile application and ensuring that it effectively addresses the needs of smallholder farmers. By collecting data directly from the field, I gained a deeper understanding of the agricultural landscape and the challenges faced by farmers, which will inform our project's design and implementation.

Community Section Development

I will also be actively involved in brainstorming and developing the community section of the mobile app, working closely with my fellow team members. Together, we'll explore ways to create a dynamic platform where farmers can connect, share experiences, and access valuable resources related to Fall Armyworm (FAW) management. Our goal is to ensure that this community section aligns with our overall plan and adds real value to the users, fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing that will help farmers combat FAW infestations more effectively. This feature will be a critical part of our project, promoting not only education but also empowerment for smallholder farmers.

FAW Detection and Diagnosis

I will be responsible for developing the FAW detection and diagnosis module of the mobile application, leveraging my expertise in machine learning and image processing. By working on this component, I aim to create a robust and accurate system that can identify early signs of FAW infestations in crops and provide timely recommendations to farmers. Using image recognition algorithms and data analysis techniques, I will develop a solution that can analyze images of crops and detect the presence of FAW damage. This module will be a key feature of our application, enabling farmers to quickly assess the health of their crops and take appropriate action to mitigate pest infestations. My work on the FAW detection and diagnosis module will help enhance the functionality and usability of our application, providing farmers with valuable insights to protect their crops and improve their yields.

Training of the model.

I will also be involved in training the machine learning model that will power the Fall Armyworm Diagnosis application. Leveraging my expertise in data science and AI, I will work on developing algorithms that can accurately detect and classify FAW infestations based on image data. By training the model on a diverse dataset of crop images and pest samples, I aim to create a robust and reliable system that can identify early signs of FAW damage and provide timely recommendations to farmers. This machine learning component will be a critical part of our application, enabling us to deliver accurate and actionable insights to users and help them mitigate the impact of FAW infestations on their crops.

System architecture of the software design document.

I will be working on the system architecture section of the software design document. In this section, I will outline the overall structure and components of the system, detailing how the various parts of the software will interact with each other. I will describe the different layers of the application, including the frontend, backend, and data storage, as well as the flow of data between these components. Additionally, I will identify the key technologies and frameworks that will be used to implement each part of the system, ensuring scalability, efficiency, and security. By contributing to this section, I will help define the foundation for the system's development and ensure that the architecture aligns with the project's goals and requirements.
